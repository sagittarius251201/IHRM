Best Practice #1: Learning as Infrastructure

Gyanodaya (Corporate University)

Global Center for Leadership Learning. Runs accelerator programs, function academies, and women's leadership initiatives like Springboard.

Gyanodaya Virtual Campus (GVC)

AI-powered, mobile-first micro-learning platform. Curates internal and external content for always-on learning.

Impact: Creates a portable learning culture, enabling rapid upskilling for tech/ESG, and driving high role-change outcomes.

Best Practice #2: Forging Future Leaders

ABG Leadership Program (ABGLP)

Structured pathways for early-career & mid-senior talent to take on complex roles across different sectors.

Global Manufacturing Leadership Program (GMLP)

Builds a dedicated pipeline of global plant leaders, with alumni moving to head units in India, China, and beyond.

Impact: Ensures a steady internal supply of culturally-aligned leaders for critical, hard-to-staff operational roles, ensuring continuity.

Best Practice #3: Career Architecture & Mobility

Career Lattice, Not Ladder

Internal mobility across roles, functions, and businesses is an explicit promise and a core part of the "One ABG" careers message.

Enabling Platforms

Tools like 'Xplore Careers' and dedicated Talent Councils proactively curate cross-business moves and developmental journeys.

Impact: A staggering 97% role-change rate over time for program participants, showcasing mobility at scale and boosting retention.

Best Practice #4: DEI with a Frontline Focus

Visible Leadership

Group-wide push for women leaders across all businesses and corporate functions.

Manufacturing Focus

Targeted stories and enablement for women in manufacturing; ERGs like WIN & WISE.

Culture Shaping

Campaigns like #ChangeTheGame actively challenge workplace bias and stereotypes.

Impact: Broadens the leadership bench, enables inclusive decision-making, and strengthens the employer brand in STEM roles.
